# Scan-And-shop


This website was created to reduce the work of carrying bags and carts in mall small shops and grocery stores.
Working of website is when a person goes for shopping, he/she just have to open the website and then scan the
product they want to buy. After scanning all the products the person just have to go to the counter and enter his id
that is assign to him/her .Then the shopkeeper will verify it and will give him option of payment .And then all this
product that have scanned by the person will be given to him,
